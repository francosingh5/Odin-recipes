Nordic Meatball recipe

Description:

The Nordic Meatball Also known as Swedish meatballs, they are made with minced beef mixed
with breadcrumbs soaked in milk and onions cut into small pieces.

these meatballs are “mum's meatballs” a catchall expression in Sweden, and favorite recipes vary widely.
Some are of the opinion that the onions should be grated in the mixture itself, while others prefer to
cut them into small pieces and fry them separately.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Mince
  • 2 tablespoons of Butter
  • 4 tablespoons of Milk
  • 4 tablespoons of Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Onion

Steps:

  1. Put the breadcrumbs in a large bowl and add the 4 tablespoons of milk.
  2. Let it absorb the liquid for about 5 minutes.
  3. In a pan, fry the chopped onion.
  4. Mix the meat with the onion, the breadcrumbs, the egg, salt and pepper.
  5. Form balls and fry in a pan with butter.
