Nordic Shortbread recipe

Description:

The Nordic Shortbread are the first cookies, there were made around the year 1100,
when the Viking culture still dominated, today they are a classic on Christmas tables.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2kg Flour
  • 250g Butter
  • 150g Powdered sugar
  • A pinch of fine Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Egg yolks
  • Lemon zest
  • 100g Chopped walnuts or almonds

Steps:

  1. Incorporate the egg, the yolks and the lemon zest, Unite everything.
  2. Add the flour and contain the ingredients without kneading.
  3. Add the nuts, integrate them and take the bun to the fridge. Leave half an hour.
  4. Stretch on the floured countertop and cut medallions or other forms.
  5. Remove, let cool and cross some with chocolate threads.
